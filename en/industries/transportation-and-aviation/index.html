Legal advisory for ground and air transportation companies operating in the Eastern Province.

A Vital Transportation and Aviation Sector Connecting the Eastern Province to the World

The Eastern Province's strategic location as an industrial and commercial gateway makes its transportation and aviation sector especially vital, from intense ground freight movement between industrial cities to King Fahd International Airport serving growing commercial and tourism traffic, creating legal complexity combining ground transport and civil aviation regulations together.

Transport Contracts and Logistics Service Agreements

We review and draft transport contracts and logistics service agreements between transport companies and industrial clients, including liability clauses for damaged or lost goods, and agreed performance standards regarding delivery timelines.

Regulatory Compliance for Airport Service Operators

We track regulatory compliance for airport service operators in the Eastern Province before the General Authority of Civil Aviation, including ground handling licenses and safety standards required in this sensitive sector.

Liability Disputes Over Transport Accidents and Delays

We represent clients in liability disputes over accidents and delays tied to ground and air transport operations, whether concerning damaged transported goods or passenger compensation for delayed or cancelled flights.

Aircraft and Ground Equipment Lease Agreements

We review aircraft lease agreements and ground equipment leases for operators in this sector, clarifying maintenance, insurance, and liability clauses at the end of the lease term or when a malfunction occurs.

Licensing for Air and Ground Freight Companies

We help new air and ground freight companies meet licensing requirements before the relevant authorities, from the General Authority of Civil Aviation for air freight to the Ministry of Transport for ground freight.

Multimodal Transport and Logistics Hubs

With the development of major logistics hub projects in the Eastern Province, we help transport companies structure multimodal transport contracts combining sea, ground, and air transport into a single supply chain.

Commercial Drone Operations

We help companies wishing to use drones for commercial purposes meet General Authority of Civil Aviation licensing requirements specific to this emerging type of air operation.

Employment Contracts for Pilots and Aviation Technicians

We help airlines draft specialized employment contracts for pilots and technicians, accounting for the special regulatory requirements on working and rest hours imposed on this sensitive job category.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our customer's shipment was damaged during air freight. Who bears liability?

We review the transport contract and air freight agreement to determine the agreed liability scope, and help you claim appropriate compensation.

Our passengers' flight was delayed for many hours. Are we automatically obligated to compensate?

It depends on the cause of delay and applicable passenger rights regulations, and we review each case's circumstances to determine actual liability.

Our aircraft lease agreement is ending soon. What are our obligations at return?

We review the required aircraft condition clauses at return and any liability for excess wear, to avoid unexpected claims from the lessor.

We are planning to establish a new air freight company. What are the basic licenses required?

We help you understand the General Authority of Civil Aviation's licensing requirements, including fleet and operational safety conditions.

One of our pilots exceeded permitted regulatory working hours. What are the legal risks?

This exposes the company to serious violations from the civil aviation authority, and we help you review your work scheduling systems to ensure compliance.

An airport ground services operator breached agreed safety standards. What are our options?

We review the service contract with them and help you claim compensation or terminate the agreement if the breach continues despite warning.
