Al-Ahsa: A Heritage Oasis With Modern Economic Reach

Agriculture forms the backbone of Al-Ahsa's historic economy, alongside growing commercial and real estate sectors in the cities of Hofuf and Mubarraz, making agricultural and real estate land disputes among the most common matters we handle there. This blend between Al-Ahsa's deep agricultural heritage and its modern urban growth creates unique legal challenges we don't see in quite the same way in Dammam or Jubail.

UNESCO's classification of Al-Ahsa as a World Heritage site imposes special restrictions on any construction or development activity within the protected oasis boundaries, and we help investors and owners understand these restrictions before undertaking any development project near protected areas.
