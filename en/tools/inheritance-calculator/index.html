Inheritance Calculator

A tool for understanding how Sharia inheritance distribution works, with a tailored review of your case from a specialist lawyer.

Sharia inheritance distribution depends on the number and degree of kinship of surviving heirs — a matter that changes fundamentally from case to case, so a generic calculator cannot give a reliable, accurate figure without reviewing each case individually. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp with your heirs' details so we can give you a precise division.

Why We Don't Offer an Automated Inheritance Calculator

Unlike the end-of-service award, each heir's Sharia-defined share changes fundamentally depending on the presence or absence of other heirs (full or partial exclusion rules), which makes any generic calculator prone to giving an inaccurate result that could mislead its user.

How We Help Instead

Send us a list of surviving heirs (spouse, children, parents, siblings if any) on WhatsApp, and we'll give you a precise division based on Sharia inheritance rules, clarifying any points requiring an official heir-determination deed.
