Qatif: Deep Agricultural and Coastal Heritage Meets Modern Growth

Qatif blends its historic agricultural character across the palm groves and Tarout Island with modern urban and commercial growth, making real estate and agricultural land disputes among the most common cases in the city. This unique blend of agricultural heritage and urban development creates legal needs unlike what we see in Dammam or Jubail.

We regularly handle agricultural land ownership and palm grove disputes, which often involve complexities around shared ownership between multiple heirs across generations, under our title deed and land dispute expertise.
