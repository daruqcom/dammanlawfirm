Family & Personal Status

Family & Personal Status Lawyers in Dammam

Personal status cases cover marriage, divorce, custody, alimony, and inheritance, heard before specialized courts under Islamic law provisions with utmost discretion and sensitivity.

Family Law Consultation on WhatsApp
Personal status is the legal field regulating family relationships: the marriage contract and its terms, divorce and khula procedures, child custody and alimony, and inheritance distribution after death. These cases are heard before specialized personal status courts, requiring handling combining legal precision with human sensitivity to family disputes' nature. Why Do Personal Status Cases Need Special Handling?

Family disputes differ from any other commercial or civil dispute; they carry a deep emotional dimension affecting parties' decisions, and often involve vulnerable parties like children needing special protection. We handle these cases with complete discretion and consideration for the psychological impact on all parties, especially children.

The Marriage Contract and Its Special Terms

We help parties draft the marriage contract including any special terms both parties wish to include, which become legally binding if they don't contradict Sharia rulings, and we clarify the full legal effect of any term before including it in the contract.

Divorce and Khula Procedures

We represent parties in divorce procedures, whether mutual consent divorce, judicial divorce for defined reasons, or khula requested by the wife, and help parties understand the financial and custody rights arising from each of these different paths.

Child Custody and Alimony

We represent parties in custody disputes, always considering the child's best interest as the fundamental standard, and help fairly assess due alimony accounting for the child's actual needs and the paying party's capacity.

Inheritance Distribution According to Sharia Rulings

We help heirs understand their legal shares in the deceased's estate, and represent them in any dispute over estate distribution or challenging a will contrary to Sharia inheritance rulings, ensuring each heir receives their full right.

Establishing or Denying Parentage

We help with cases establishing or denying parentage, sensitive cases needing precise legal handling and adequate documentation, considering these cases' deep effect on the child's identity and future.

Wills and Family Endowments

We help clients draft legally valid wills within permitted Sharia limits, and establish family endowments to preserve family assets across generations, achieving the testator's goals without violating mandatory inheritance rulings.

Family Mediation as a Litigation Alternative

When there's an opportunity to preserve the family relationship despite disagreement, we help parties turn to family mediation to reach amicable solutions, especially in custody disputes that particularly benefit from avoiding full judicial escalation.

Spousal Alimony Cases and Arrears

We help wives claim due spousal alimony and its arrears, and represent husbands in fairly assessing alimony accounting for their actual income and living circumstances, avoiding unrealistic claims from either party.

Family Cases for Those Residing Outside the Kingdom

We help families with a member residing outside the Kingdom understand additional procedures necessary to ensure any family judgment's enforceability in both countries, a matter sometimes needing precise coordination with international bodies.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm considering divorce but worried about my children's custody. How do you help me?

We help you understand actual custody standards and represent you in a way considering your children's best interest, with a realistic clarification of your legal position from the start.

My wife filed a khula lawsuit. What are my financial and custody rights in this case?

We review your case's details and clarify the rights arising from khula specifically, which may differ from your rights in an ordinary divorce case.

My father passed away and we need help dividing the inheritance among heirs. How do you help us?

We determine the correct legal shares for each heir and help you complete the division procedures formally, representing you in any dispute if disagreement arises among heirs.

I want to include a special term in my marriage contract. Is this legally possible?

Yes, as long as the term doesn't contradict Sharia rulings, and we help you draft it with legal precision ensuring its enforceability when needed.

Can an amicable agreement on custody and alimony be reached without lengthy litigation?

Yes in many cases through family mediation, and we help you explore this option first before escalating to full litigation.

I want to write a will preserving an additional share for one of my children. Is this possible?

A will has certain Sharia limits that cannot be exceeded, and we help you understand these limits and draft a legally valid will achieving your wish as much as possible.

A family member of mine resides outside the Kingdom. How do we ensure any family judgment is enforceable in both countries?

We help you understand the additional procedures necessary, which may need coordination with international bodies to ensure the judgment's recognition in both countries.
