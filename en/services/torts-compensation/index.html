Torts & Compensation

Torts & Compensation Legal Services

Fair compensation claims for physical, financial, and reputational damages.

Torts and compensation law in Dammam covers claiming compensation for damages from accidents or breach of obligation. If you've suffered damage warranting compensation, the next step is reaching out on WhatsApp to assess your claim's value.

Compensation Is Not a Legal Luxury, It Is Repairing the Harm

The idea behind our torts practice is simple: whoever is harmed by another's act should not bear it alone. But turning that idea into an awarded amount requires organized proof of the damage and causation and a realistic valuation of the claim, and that is exactly what we handle for you from the first document through to enforcing the judgment.

Traffic Accidents and Compensation

Our most frequent files are traffic accidents and compensation: claims against insurers for vehicle repair and downtime and for bodily injuries, and pushing back on low settlement offers designed to close the file before the true scale of the damage becomes clear, especially on Dammam's highways and industrial corridors.

Bodily Injuries From Every Cause

Beyond road accidents, we handle personal and bodily injury compensation claims arising from negligence in public or private facilities, construction accidents, or assault, coordinating where needed with our work injury team when the harm occurs in a workplace and multiple parties may be liable.

Medical Malpractice

Medical malpractice compensation files are among the most delicate we handle, combining human tragedy with complex technical proof before specialized panels. We build the file on the complete medical record and an independent consultant's opinion before filing, because the strength of the start usually determines the outcome at the end.

Diyah and Arsh: The Sharia Track for Life and Bodily Harm

In death and serious injury cases the Diyah and Arsh track applies with its fixed Sharia amounts, often intersecting with a pending criminal case in homicide or manslaughter. We represent the deceased's family or the injured party in pursuing the private right within the criminal case or through a separate claim, whichever serves them better.

Material and Moral Damages

Harm is not limited to the body; a financial loss caused by another's breach, or defamation that damaged your reputation, are both compensable under material and moral damages claims, and where the defamation is a cybercrime we coordinate the civil and criminal tracks together.

Realistic Valuation Before Big Promises

We will not promise you a fantasy figure to win your engagement; we read your file and tell you the realistic range courts award in comparable cases, and when settlement beats litigation. That candor saves you years of waiting for a judgment that would not even cover the cost of claiming it.

How We Start With You

Send a WhatsApp summary of the incident with the core documents: the accident report or medical file, proof of expenses and lost income, and any correspondence with the other party or the insurer. We give you an initial assessment of your position and the most suitable claim track, with clear costs before any commitment, and do not sign any release before then.

Frequently Asked Questions

I was in a traffic accident that was not my fault. What are my first steps?

Keep the traffic or Najm report, medical injury reports, and repair invoices, since these documents are the foundation of the claim, then contact us before accepting any settlement offer from the insurance company.

Can I claim compensation for moral damage in Saudi Arabia?

Yes, Saudi courts award moral damages in cases such as defamation and serious injuries once the harm and causation are proven, and we help you value your claim realistically.

What is the difference between Diyah, Arsh, and civil compensation?

Diyah is the fixed Sharia amount for loss of life, Arsh is the assessed amount for lesser injuries, while civil compensation covers additional losses such as lost earnings and treatment costs; the tracks can combine in a single file.

A doctor made an error in my treatment. How do I prove it?

Proving medical malpractice rests on the complete medical file and a specialized expert opinion, and these claims are heard by dedicated panels, so the sooner you preserve reports and request a copy of your file, the stronger your position.

The insurance company offered less than my actual loss. Should I accept?

Do not sign a final release before showing it to us; signing usually extinguishes your right to any later claim even if the damage turns out greater than it first appeared.

How long does a typical compensation file take?

Direct insurance claims can resolve within weeks, while court compensation cases usually take several months, and longer where technical or medical expert reports are needed.
