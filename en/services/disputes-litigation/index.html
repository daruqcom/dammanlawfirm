Disputes & Litigation Legal Services

Direct representation before commercial and labor courts and arbitration panels, with a clear method from the first hearing to final judgment.

Disputes and litigation law in Dammam covers representation before commercial and labor courts and commercial arbitration — the last line when amicable settlement fails. If you're party to an existing or anticipated dispute, the next step is reaching out immediately on WhatsApp for a position assessment.

When a Business Relationship Reaches a Dead End

Not every dispute needs a courtroom, but when amicable settlement fails, the difference between a strong position and a lost one usually comes down to the quality of representation from the first hearing. Our disputes and litigation practice manages the file with a single method from drafting the first memorandum through to enforcing the final judgment.

Litigation and Advocacy Before the Courts

We represent you in litigation and advocacy before commercial, labor, and general courts, managing the full file from drafting or responding to the statement of claim through oral pleadings and judgment.

Arbitration as a Contractual Alternative to Litigation

When your contract contains an arbitration clause, or when the parties to a dispute prefer a faster and more confidential track, we represent you in arbitration proceedings, from appointing arbitrators and drafting the statement of claim through to the award and its ratification.

Enforcement: When the Judgment Debtor Refuses to Comply

A judgment in your favor has no value without actual enforcement. We handle enforcement before the Enforcement Court, from freezing bank accounts and assets to requesting a travel ban, to make sure your right is realized in practice, not just on paper.

International and Cross-Border Disputes

For Eastern Province businesses dealing with parties outside the Kingdom, we handle international and cross-border disputes, including determining the proper jurisdiction, serving foreign parties, and enforcing foreign judgments inside the Kingdom and vice versa.

Mediation and Alternative Dispute Resolution

Before entering lengthy litigation, we assess with you the viability of mediation and alternative dispute resolution, especially in commercial disputes between parties who want the relationship to continue, where serious mediation resolves many disagreements at far lower cost and time than court.

Expert Testimony and Judicial Expertise

Many commercial and engineering cases are settled based on a court-appointed technical expert's report. We help with expert testimony by preparing memoranda that support your position before the expert, and challenging the report if it proves inaccurate.

Electronic Litigation and Legal Drafting

We use e-filing platforms to follow up your cases remotely without requiring your repeated attendance, and handle drafting legal memoranda and pleadings with the precision that accounts for every detail that can turn a case's outcome.

How We Start With You

Send us the statement of claim on WhatsApp if you are the defendant, or a summary of your dispute if you are considering filing, along with any relevant contract or correspondence. We assess your position candidly, including the chances of success, cost, and expected timeline, before any commitment to engage us.

Frequently Asked Questions

I received a lawsuit and don't know where to start. What is the first step?

Do not delay your response; send us the statement of claim immediately, since hearing dates and response deadlines are fixed by law, and we build the defense memorandum within the available window.

What is the difference between arbitration and court litigation?

Arbitration is faster and more confidential, and the parties choose their own arbitrators, but it requires a prior agreement in the contract or a later agreement between the parties, while litigation is the default track when no arbitration clause exists.

A judgment was issued in my favor, but the other party is not complying. What do I do?

We file an enforcement request before the Enforcement Court, which holds broad powers such as freezing accounts and assets and imposing a travel ban to compel the debtor to comply.

My opponent in the dispute is based or registered outside Saudi Arabia. Can they still be sued?

Yes, with additional considerations around jurisdiction, serving the foreign party, and later enforcing the judgment in their country, and we assess these factors with you before starting proceedings.

Is mediation a serious option or just a formality before litigation?

Serious mediation saves significant time and cost when both parties genuinely want a practical resolution, and we help you assess whether the chances of settlement are real before entering into it.

My case needs a technical expert opinion. How is that handled?

We recommend and deal with court-appointed experts, and prepare the technical memoranda that support your position before the expert report, since that report often determines the direction of the judgment.

Facing an existing or anticipated dispute?

Reach out immediately to assess your legal position before the first hearing.
