International & Cross-Border Disputes

International & Cross-border Disputes Lawyers in Dammam

International disputes require precise understanding of more than one legal system, especially when parties are of different nationalities or contracts are executed across more than one country simultaneously.

International Dispute Consultation on WhatsApp
International and cross-border disputes are disputes involving a multi-jurisdictional legal dimension, whether due to parties' different nationalities, contract execution across more than one country, or disputed assets existing in multiple countries. This type of dispute needs coordination between different legal systems, and sometimes local lawyers in relevant countries, to ensure comprehensive legal coverage. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp to discuss your international dispute.

Determining the Applicable Law in an International Dispute

Among the first challenges in any international dispute is determining which legal system governs the dispute, and we help you analyze your contract's applicable law clause if one exists, or determine applicable international rules in its absence.

Determining the Competent Judicial or Arbitral Body

Alongside the applicable law, we help you determine the competent judicial or arbitral body for hearing the dispute, whether a Saudi or foreign court, or an international arbitration tribunal agreed upon in the contract.

Coordinating With Local Lawyers in Other Countries

When the dispute requires local expertise in another legal system, we coordinate with trusted law firms in relevant countries, ensuring comprehensive legal coverage accounting for the specifics of each legal system relevant to the dispute.

Enforcing Judgments and Awards Across Borders

When a judgment or arbitration award is issued in one country, you might need to enforce it in another country where the other party's assets exist, and we help you follow recognition and enforcement procedures per relevant international agreements.

International Commercial Disputes Between Suppliers and Distributors

We represent local companies in their disputes with international suppliers or distributors, including disputes over goods quality, delivery delays, or breach of agreed regional exclusivity terms.

Cross-Border Investment Disputes

We help investors in disputes related to cross-border investments, whether they're foreign investors in Saudi Arabia facing a local dispute, or Saudi investors facing a dispute abroad.

Challenges of Gathering Evidence and Coordination Across Borders

International disputes carry additional challenges in gathering evidence and coordinating testimony across international borders, and we help you manage this logistical complexity efficiently while preserving the strength of your legal position.

Amicable Settlement as an Option in International Disputes

Given full international litigation's complexity and cost, we often recommend exploring amicable settlement or international mediation first, especially when continuing the commercial relationship benefits both parties despite the existing dispute.

Choosing the Right International Arbitration Seat

When your contract stipulates international arbitration, choosing the arbitration seat fundamentally affects applicable procedures and the ease of later enforcing the decision, and we help you carefully assess this choice before signing any new contract.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our contract with a foreign company doesn't specify applicable law in a dispute. How do we determine it?

We analyze applicable private international law rules to determine the most suitable system, relying on factors like place of performance and parties' nationality.

We have a judgment issued by a Saudi court and want to enforce it in another country where the other party's assets exist. What's the procedure?

We help you follow judgment recognition and local enforcement procedures in that country per relevant international agreements.

Our international supplier breached agreed regional exclusivity terms. What are our options?

We review your contract's exclusivity clause and help you claim a halt to this breach or compensation, coordinating with local lawyers if necessary.

We need local legal expertise in another country for part of our dispute. Do you coordinate with lawyers there?

Yes, we coordinate with trusted law firms in relevant countries to ensure comprehensive legal coverage accounting for each system's specifics.

Is amicable settlement a realistic option in complex international disputes?

Yes, and we often recommend exploring it first given full international litigation's complexity and cost, especially if continuing the relationship benefits both parties.

How do we gather evidence and testimony from parties located in another country?

We help you manage this logistical challenge, coordinating with relevant authorities and local lawyers to ensure this evidence's legal acceptance.

We're drafting a new contract with a foreign party stipulating international arbitration. What's the most important consideration when choosing the arbitration seat?

Ease of later enforcing the decision in the country where the other party's assets exist matters most, and we help you carefully assess this choice.
