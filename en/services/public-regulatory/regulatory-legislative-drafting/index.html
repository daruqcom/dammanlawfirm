Regulatory & Legislative Drafting

Regulatory & Legislative Drafting Lawyers in Dammam

Drafting internal policies and regulatory compliance protects your business from costly violations, especially in closely regulated sectors like energy, finance, and healthcare.

Regulatory Drafting Consultation on WhatsApp
Regulatory and legislative drafting is the service of designing a business's internal policies and bylaws in compliance with applicable government regulations in its sector, building internal mechanisms ensuring ongoing compliance with these requirements, not just formal documentation. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp to discuss your business's regulatory needs.

Why Do Businesses Need Formally Documented Internal Policies?

Undocumented internal policies leave a business exposed to conflicting interpretations and inconsistent application by employees, increasing the risk of unintentional regulatory violations. Formal documentation ensures unified understanding and demonstrable compliance before any regulatory body.

Drafting HR Policies Compliant With Labor Law

We help businesses draft internal HR policies compliant with Saudi labor law, including leave, discipline, and service termination policies, avoiding violations that could arise from inconsistent application of these policies.

Regulatory Compliance for Heavily Regulated Sectors

Sectors like energy, finance, and healthcare are subject to complex regulatory frameworks, and we help businesses operating in these sectors draft internal compliance policies practically covering every regulatory requirement in a way applicable daily.

Personal Data Protection Policies

With the Personal Data Protection Law's full enforcement taking effect, we help businesses draft internal policies for collecting, processing, and storing personal data compliant with this law's requirements, avoiding violations that could cost substantial fines.

Anti-Money-Laundering Regulations for Financial Institutions

Financial institutions subject to anti-money-laundering requirements need precise internal policies for customer due diligence and suspicious transaction reporting, and we help them draft these policies meeting SAMA's or the relevant regulator's requirements.

Corporate Codes of Ethical Conduct

We help businesses draft codes of ethical conduct defining expected behavior standards from employees and management, including conflict of interest and anti-bribery policies, documents that have become an essential requirement for good corporate governance.

Periodic Policy Review as Regulations Update

Government regulations continuously update, and we help businesses establish a periodic review mechanism for their internal policies to ensure ongoing compliance with any new regulatory amendments, instead of relying on outdated policies time may have overtaken.

Internal Training on Policy Implementation

Drafting the policy alone isn't enough without actual employee training on understanding and implementing it, and we help businesses design internal training programs ensuring employees genuinely absorb new policies and correctly apply them in their daily work.

Translating Policies Into Arabic and English With Legal Precision

For multinational businesses, we help draft policies in two legally matching languages, avoiding any conflicting interpretation that could arise from an imprecise translation of a sensitive clause in the original policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our business doesn't have formally documented HR policies. What are the actual risks of this situation?

The absence of formal documentation increases risks of inconsistent application and unintentional violations, and we help you draft clear policies protecting your business.

Do we need a separate internal policy for personal data protection, or is compliance with the general law enough?

We recommend a dedicated internal policy translating the law's requirements into daily practical procedures, significantly reducing violation risk.

Our business is financial and needs to comply with anti-money-laundering requirements. How do you help us?

We help you draft customer due diligence and suspicious transaction reporting policies precisely meeting the relevant regulator's requirements.

Is a code of ethical conduct statutorily mandatory, or just good governance practice?

Mandatory status varies by sector, but it's in all cases an essential governance practice we help you adopt reflecting your business's actual values.

How often should our internal policies be reviewed to ensure compliance with new regulations?

We recommend at least an annual periodic review, with an immediate review upon any major regulatory amendment concerning your sector.

We drafted internal policies but employees aren't applying them correctly. What's the solution?

We help you design an internal training program ensuring employees genuinely absorb the policies and correctly apply them in their daily work.

Our business is multinational and needs its policies in two languages. How do we ensure their legal alignment?

We help you draft both versions with matching legal precision, avoiding any conflicting interpretation arising from an imprecise translation of a sensitive clause.
