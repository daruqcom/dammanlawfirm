Government Contracts & Public Procurement

Government Contracts & Public Procurement Lawyers in Dammam

Contracting with government bodies is governed by the Government Tenders and Procurement Law, with special terms and procedures fundamentally different from ordinary commercial contracts in deadlines and accountability.

Government Contract Consultation on WhatsApp
Government contracts are contracts concluded between contractors and suppliers on one hand and government bodies on the other, governed by the Government Tenders and Procurement Law imposing precise procedures for submission, award, and contract execution. Any contractor seeking to contract with the government sector needs precise understanding of this system fundamentally different from ordinary commercial contracting. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp to review your tender or government contract.

Reviewing Tender Documents Before Submission

We precisely review tender documents before submission, looking for any qualification condition that could exclude your bid procedurally, and help you prepare a bid fully compliant with the government entity's technical and financial requirements.

Submission and Award Procedures

We follow the complete tender submission procedure, from preparing required documents to submitting the bid within the statutory deadline, and help you understand award standards and how to strengthen your bid's competitive chances.

Appealing Exclusion or Award Decisions

When your business is excluded from a tender or a competitor wins the award in a way you feel is unfair, we help you file a formal grievance within the statutory deadline, relying on any procedural or substantive defect in the government entity's decision.

Delay Penalty Clauses and Performance Guarantees

We review delay penalty clauses and performance guarantees in the government contract, and help you understand their calculation and activation mechanism, avoiding unexpected financial surprises during contract execution.

Government Contract Execution Disputes

We represent contractors and suppliers in any dispute arising with the government entity over contract execution or interpretation of a contractual clause, whether concerning delayed payments, scope modification, or rejection of completed work.

Local Content Requirements and the Taqafu Program

We help suppliers and contractors understand local content requirements and the Taqafu local content preference program, a standard increasingly affecting major government tender awards in the Kingdom.

Public-Private Partnership Contracts

We help investors wishing to enter public-private partnership projects understand the risk allocation structure between the government body and the private investor, growing contractual models in the Kingdom.

Collecting From Government Bodies When Payment Is Delayed

When a government body delays paying your contractual dues, we help you pursue collection through the appropriate regulatory channels for this type of contract, procedurally different from collecting from an ordinary commercial entity.

Performance and Advance Payment Guarantees

We help contractors and suppliers understand the mechanism for recovering bank guarantees submitted to the government body after execution completes, and how to act if the body refuses to release them without statutorily justified cause.

How We Start With You

Send us the tender, government contract, or dispute details you're facing on WhatsApp. We assess your position quickly, since appeal deadlines in this sector are often short and cannot tolerate delay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our bid was excluded from a government tender without clear cause. What are our options?

We review the exclusion grounds and help you file a formal grievance within the statutory period if the exclusion was unjustified.

The government entity delayed paying our contractual dues for months. What's the appropriate action?

We review your contract to determine the available regulatory claim mechanism, and pursue collection through channels appropriate for this type of contract.

Our government contract includes a very high delay penalty. Can we object to it?

We review whether the penalty is proportionate to the actual damage, and assess the possibility of negotiating a reduction or challenging its application if the delay wasn't attributable to you.

What local content requirements must be met to qualify for a major government tender?

We help you understand the Taqafu program's precise requirements applying to your sector, standards increasingly affecting award chances.

The government entity refused to accept work we're executing citing non-conformity. Is that justified?

We review contractual specifications and inspection reports, and help you object if the rejection isn't based on sound technical grounds.

We're planning to enter a public-private partnership project. What's the most important clause to review?

The risk allocation structure between the parties matters most, and we help you assess it precisely before committing to any project.

We completed contract execution but the government body refuses to release our performance guarantee. What are our options?

We help you claim release of the guarantee based on completing agreed work, escalating formally if the refusal continues without statutory cause.
