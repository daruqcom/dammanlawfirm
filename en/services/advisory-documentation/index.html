Advisory & Documentation Legal Services

Precise drafting and documentation of contracts and legal papers that prevent disputes before they happen.

Advisory and documentation services in Dammam cover contract drafting, power-of-attorney documentation, and preventive legal review of documents. If you need a legal document drafted or reviewed, the next step is reaching out on WhatsApp.

Legal Prevention Is Always Cheaper Than Curing a Dispute

Most cases that reach court could have been avoided with a clearer contract or a legal review that preceded signing. Our advisory and documentation practice is dedicated to this preventive stage: precise drafting, sound notarization, and a clear legal opinion before a decision is made, not after harm has occurred.

Contract Drafting and Review

We handle contract drafting and review across every type, from supply and service agreements to partnership arrangements, looking for any ambiguous or unbalanced clause that could later turn into a costly dispute.

Ongoing Legal Consultations

For business owners who need a ready legal reference without hiring an in-house team, we provide legal consultations on a periodic or as-needed basis, covering every business decision with a regulatory dimension.

Document Notarization and Attestation

We help with notarizing and attesting documents and powers of attorney through the Najiz platform and notaries public, ensuring their full legal effect whether used inside the Kingdom or abroad.

Najiz Services and Electronic Powers of Attorney

As legal procedures shift to digital platforms, we handle Najiz and electronic power-of-attorney services, from issuing a remote e-POA to following up your transactions without requiring your personal attendance.

Due Diligence and Business Intelligence

Before any acquisition, partnership, or major investment, we conduct due diligence and business intelligence on the other party, uncovering hidden legal or financial risks before you sign any commitment.

Legal Opinions and Research

For decisions requiring documented legal backing, we prepare legal opinions and research built on careful analysis of the relevant regulations, giving you a clear basis for any strategic decision.

Setting Up an In-House Legal Department

For growing companies needing an internal legal team, we help with setting up an in-house legal department, from designing the structure and authorities to training the initial team.

Legal Training for Business Teams

We provide legal training for sales, HR, and procurement teams, equipping them to recognize common legal risks in their daily work before those risks become a problem.

Certified Legal Translation

We provide certified legal translation for contracts and official documents between Arabic and English, with precision that preserves legal meaning rather than a purely literal rendering.

Settlement as a Faster Alternative to Litigation

Before escalating to a lawsuit, we help you explore settlement as a faster, lower-cost option, especially when both parties genuinely want to preserve the business relationship.

How We Start With You

Frequently Asked Questions

Is reviewing a contract before signing worth the cost if it looks simple?

Yes, most disputes we later handle originate from a clause that seemed simple at signing, and reviewing a contract upfront is far cheaper than the cost of subsequent litigation.

How do I notarize a power of attorney for someone outside the Kingdom?

It can be notarized through the Saudi embassy or consulate in their country of residence, then attested locally, and we handle these procedures from start until the power of attorney becomes effective.

I need a documented legal opinion before a major investment decision. Do you provide that?

Yes, we prepare legal opinion memoranda based on careful research of the relevant regulations, supporting your decision with a clear legal position you can reference later.

Do you help startups that have no in-house legal department?

Yes, we provide ongoing outsourced legal management, so the company gets contract review and periodic advisory without the cost of hiring a full internal legal team.

Is pre-litigation settlement a serious option or a waste of time?

When professionally managed, settlement saves significant time and cost and preserves the business relationship, and we help you assess its viability before entering into it.

Do you handle foreign-language documents needing certified translation?

Yes, we provide certified legal translation for contracts and official documents, ensuring the translation reflects the precise legal meaning rather than a purely literal rendering.
