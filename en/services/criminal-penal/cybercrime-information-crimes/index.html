Cybercrime & Information Crimes

Cybercrime & Information Crimes Lawyers in Dammam

Cybercrime includes hacking, online fraud, unauthorized system access, and publishing harmful content, governed by the Anti-Cyber Crime Law with relatively strict penalties.

Cybercrime Consultation on WhatsApp
Cybercrime covers a wide range of statutorily criminalized acts: unauthorized access to a system or electronic account, financial fraud online, defamation or publishing harmful content, and theft or destruction of digital data. Whether you're accused in a cybercrime case or a victim of hacking or online fraud, the first step is acting quickly. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp to review your case.

The Scope of Cybercrime Under Saudi Law

The Anti-Cyber Crime Law covers multiple acts: illegal access to a site or electronic system, intercepting data during transmission, destroying or modifying data without authorization, plus other crimes like online defamation and online financial fraud.

Defending the Accused in Hacking Cases

We defend the accused in hacking and unauthorized access cases, through precise review of presented digital evidence and how legally it was collected, since any digital evidence gathered without a valid judicial warrant could be challengeable in its legality.

Representing Victims of Hacking and Online Fraud

We help victims of hacking or online fraud file a formal documented report, guiding them to preserve digital evidence intact without intervention that could corrupt it, a critical step for the success of any subsequent perpetrator pursuit.

Online Financial Fraud and Phishing

Financial fraud cases through phishing sites or messages are widespread, and we help victims act quickly with the bank to try recovering the stolen amount, alongside a criminal report against the party responsible for the fraud.

Defamation and Harmful Content on Social Media

Publishing defamatory or offensive content through social media platforms constitutes a cybercrime, and we help affected parties document this content and file a formal report, requesting its removal from the platform alongside the criminal action.

Cybercrime for Commercial Businesses

Commercial businesses face special risks such as their systems being hacked or their sensitive commercial data being stolen, and we help them handle these incidents quickly, from formal reporting to assessing potential liability toward customers affected by leaked data.

Protecting Digital Evidence and Its Chain of Custody

The success of any cybercrime case, whether defense or prosecution, heavily depends on the integrity of the digital evidence chain of custody from the moment of discovery through to court presentation. We help clients precisely document this chain to avoid any challenge to the evidence's credibility.

Unauthorized Access by Former Employees

A recurring situation is a former employee accessing their previous employer's systems without authorization after their employment ends, and we help businesses handle these cases, from establishing unauthorized access to assessing the resulting damage.

Online Extortion and Threats to Publish Private Content

The crime of online extortion, such as threatening to publish private photos or content in exchange for money or other demands, is among the most psychologically damaging cybercrimes for victims. We help victims handle this threat with complete confidentiality and speed, documenting all communication with the perpetrator as evidence.

Protecting Startups From Digital Intellectual Property Theft

Technology startups are especially exposed to risks of their source code or user data being stolen, and we help them establish preventive security and legal procedures, alongside acting quickly when any actual breach incident occurs.

How We Start With You

Send us the incident details, whether you're accused or a victim, along with any digital evidence you have on WhatsApp. We help you act with necessary speed, since speed is a decisive factor in cybercrime cases.

Frequently Asked Questions

A breach of my online account was discovered. What are the correct first steps?

Change passwords immediately and document everything possible of the breach evidence, then contact us to file a formal documented report.

I received an accusation of unauthorized access to a certain system. What's my first defense?

We review the legality of the digital evidence presented against you and how it was collected, since any flaw in these procedures could significantly weaken the prosecution's position.

I fell victim to financial fraud through a phishing message. Can the stolen amount be recovered?

We help you act immediately with the bank to try stopping or recovering the transfer, alongside filing a criminal report against the party responsible.

Someone is publishing offensive content about me on social media platforms. What are my options?

We help you document this content and file a formal report, requesting its removal from the platform itself alongside the criminal action.

We discovered a breach of our company's systems and potential customer data leak. What are our obligations?

We help you report immediately and assess your liability toward affected customers, while taking damage containment measures as quickly as possible.

A former employee is still accessing our systems without authorization after their employment ended. How do we prove that?

We help you document and analyze access logs to establish unauthorized access, the basis for any subsequent report or lawsuit against them.
