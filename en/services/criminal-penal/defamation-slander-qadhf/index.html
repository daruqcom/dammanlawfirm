Defamation, Slander & Qadhf

Defamation & Slander Lawyers in Dammam

Defamation and slander involve harming a person's reputation through false accusations, whether spoken or via today's widespread social media platforms, and can be pursued both criminally and civilly simultaneously.

Defamation and slander are two crimes related to harming a person's reputation by attributing a false incident or accusation to them, whether done verbally, in writing, or via today's widespread social media platforms. Qadhf specifically has a distinct nature in Islamic jurisprudence with defined rulings, related specifically to accusing someone of a certain crime like adultery without proof, while defamation covers a broader scope of reputational harm. The Legal Difference Between Qadhf and Defamation

Qadhf has a distinct nature in Islamic jurisprudence, specifically related to accusing someone of a certain crime like adultery without proof, with defined rulings and penalties. Defamation is broader in scope and covers any harm to a person's reputation, social standing, or professional standing without correct basis.

Representing Defamation and Slander Victims

We help affected parties precisely document the defamation or slander incident, and determine the most suitable claim path, whether through a criminal report or a civil lawsuit claiming compensation, or both together based on the case's circumstances.

Defamation Through Social Media Platforms

With social media's spread, digital defamation has become especially common, and we help victims document the offensive content and file a formal report, requesting its removal from the platform alongside legal action.

Defending an Accused in an Unsubstantiated Qadhf or Defamation Case

We defend the accused in cases not actually established, through precise review of presented evidence and clarifying any legitimate defense, such as the claimed incident's truth or the absence of actual intent to harm.

Civil Compensation for Moral and Material Damage

We help victims claim comprehensive compensation for moral damage resulting from harm to their reputation, plus any tangible material damage like commercial loss or a job opportunity resulting from this defamation.

Defaming Commercial Businesses and Trademarks

Defamation isn't limited to individuals; a commercial business or trademark could be subjected to defamation harming its commercial reputation, and we help these businesses claim their rights with the same mechanisms available to individuals.

Distinguishing Defamation From Legitimate Criticism

We help you understand the precise distinction between unlawful defamation and legitimate criticism or opinion expression, since this distinction is fundamental to determining whether the act warrants legal accountability or falls within legitimate free expression.

Speed of Action in Digital Defamation Cases

We advise quick action in online defamation cases, since digital content's rapid spread increases damage's scale the longer it remains published, and early action limits this spread.

Defamation From a Former Employee or Business Competitor

We help business owners subjected to defamation from a disgruntled former employee or a business competitor seeking to harm their reputation distinguish these special-motive cases from ordinary defamation cases, building a suitable strategy for each.
