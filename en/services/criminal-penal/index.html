Criminal & Penal Legal Services

Firm criminal defense and representation before penal courts, in matters touching individuals and businesses alike.

Criminal and penal law in Dammam covers defending the accused and representing victims before penal courts and the Public Prosecution. If you're party to a criminal matter, the next step is reaching out urgently on WhatsApp.

In Criminal Cases, Time and the First Step Determine the Entire Outcome

There is no room for delay in penal matters; the first hours after arrest or a summons for questioning often determine the entire direction of the case. We handle these files with immediate readiness, whether you are an accused needing a firm defense or a victim seeking to recover your right.

Criminal Defense

We handle criminal defense for the accused at every stage of the case, from attending initial questioning by the Public Prosecution, to pleading before the Penal Court, through to appeal stages if needed.

Release, Bail, and Dropping Private Rights

We help explore release, bail, and dropping private rights options, including negotiating settlement or waiver with the victim where legally possible and available for the nature of the crime.

Cybercrime and Online Blackmail

We represent victims of cybercrime and online blackmail and extortion, documenting digital evidence and filing reports with the competent authorities, acting quickly to limit ongoing harm.

Fraud, Scams, and Financial Crimes

We handle fraud and scam, bribery and money laundering, and white-collar and corporate crime cases, whether representing the accused or the victim seeking to recover their funds.

Forgery and Defamation

We represent parties in forgery and defamation and slander cases, crimes often tied to serious damage to commercial or personal reputation that demands swift action.

Assault, Affray, and Harassment

We handle defense or victim representation in assault and affray and harassment and moral cases, with particular sensitivity to their personal and social impact on the parties.

Drug, Alcohol, and Weapons Cases

We provide specialized defense in drug, alcohol and intoxicants, and weapons cases, with careful examination of the soundness of the arrest and search procedures that could invalidate evidence if flawed.

Homicide and Qisas

In homicide and qisas cases, we represent the deceased's family in pursuing the private right or Diyah, or provide a firm defense for the accused, including establishing mitigating circumstances or negating criminal intent.

Juvenile Cases

We represent minors in juvenile cases before the competent bodies with procedures that account for their age, aiming to protect their future and avoid a criminal record that follows them long-term.

Contact us immediately on WhatsApp for any summons, arrest, or report filed against or on behalf of you; speed is decisive in these cases. We assess your position within hours and act immediately where urgent attendance is needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

I was summoned for questioning by the Public Prosecution. Should I bring a lawyer?

Strongly yes; you have the statutory right to have a lawyer present during questioning, and having one present from the first moment protects you from answers that could unintentionally be used against you later.

A relative of mine was arrested. What is the first step to take?

Contact us immediately with the detention details and location; we act quickly to determine the reason for detention, communicate with the competent authority, and follow up on release or bail procedures if available.

Can the private right in a criminal case be dropped after it is filed?

Yes, in many crimes involving a private right, the victim can waive or settle, which can mitigate the penalty or end the case in some situations, and we negotiate on your behalf in a way that serves your interest.

I am being blackmailed or threatened online. What should I do?

Do not comply with the demands or delete the conversations or evidence, and contact us immediately to document the evidence and file a formal report with the cybercrime authorities.

Does defending someone accused of a crime automatically mean seeking their acquittal?

No, the lawyer's role is ensuring a fair trial and confirming the soundness of the procedures and the sufficiency of the evidence, and the defense may end in a reduced sentence or establishing mitigating circumstances even without a full acquittal.

My son is a minor accused of a violation. Are juvenile cases tried like adult cases?

No, juvenile cases are heard before specialized bodies with different procedures that account for the accused's age and aim for rehabilitation rather than pure punishment, and we represent minors in a way that protects their future as much as possible.
