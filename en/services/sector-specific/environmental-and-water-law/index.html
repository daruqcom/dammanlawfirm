Environmental & Water Law

Environmental & Water Lawyers in Dammam

Precise environmental compliance for Eastern Province industrial operators, from emissions permits to water treatment and industrial waste management.

Environmental Law Consultation on WhatsApp
Environmental and water law covers emissions permits, industrial waste treatment, water treatment obligations, and compliance with environmental protection regulations issued by relevant authorities. The Eastern Province hosts a high density of industrial facilities and refineries, making precise environmental compliance vital to avoid costly violations or operational shutdown. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp to review your environmental status.

Emissions Permits for Industrial Facilities

We review air emissions permit applications for industrial facilities and refineries, confirming compliance with required environmental standards before actual operation starts, avoiding any violation arising from exceeding permitted limits.

Responding to Environmental Violation Notices

When the relevant authority issues an environmental violation notice, we help businesses respond within the defined statutory period, with precise review of the violation's grounds and assessing objection possibility.

Industrial Discharge Permits and Water Treatment

We review industrial discharge permits for water used in industrial operations, and help businesses meet requirements for treating this water before discharge per approved environmental standards.

Managing Industrial and Hazardous Waste

We help factories and refineries understand their obligations related to managing industrial and hazardous waste, from safe storage to proper disposal, coordinating with licensed bodies for this type of service.

Environmental Impact Studies for New Projects

We help investors wishing to establish new industrial facilities meet prior environmental impact study requirements, a mandatory step before obtaining any construction or operating license.

Compliance With Groundwater and Surface Water Quality Standards

We help facilities whose operations might affect groundwater or surface water quality understand their special regulatory obligations, especially in areas near sensitive water sources.

Liability for Environmental Damage and Pollution

We help parties harmed by environmental pollution resulting from a neighboring facility's activity claim compensation for resulting damage, determining the responsible party based on precise technical evidence.

Challenging Environmental Fine Decisions

When an environmental fine is imposed that a business believes is disproportionate to the actual violation, we help it challenge this decision before the relevant authority, relying on precise technical assessment of the alleged violation.

Periodic Environmental Compliance Reviews for Existing Factories

We advise existing industrial businesses to conduct periodic environmental compliance reviews rather than relying solely on the initial permit obtained at founding; environmental standards change over time, and a business that doesn't regularly review its compliance may discover an accumulated violation it wasn't previously aware of.

Environmental Disclosure in Industrial Acquisition Deals

When an investor acquires an existing industrial facility, we help them examine that facility's historical environmental obligations as part of due diligence, avoiding inheriting undisclosed environmental liability after the deal completes, coordinating with our mergers and acquisitions services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do when receiving an environmental violation notice?

You must respond within the specified statutory period — we help you draft the response and review the violation's grounds and assess objection possibility.

Do you review industrial discharge permits before operations begin?

Yes, we review environmental permit applications before any new industrial facility starts operating to ensure all required standards are met.

We're planning to build a new factory. Do we need a prior environmental impact study?

Yes, this is a mandatory step before obtaining any construction or operating license, and we help you meet its requirements.

How do we handle hazardous waste resulting from our industrial operations?

We help you understand safe storage and proper disposal obligations, coordinating with bodies licensed for this type of service.

A neighboring facility caused pollution harming our agricultural land. What are our options?

We help you claim compensation for resulting damage, determining the responsible party based on precise technical evidence.

We were imposed an environmental fine we believe is disproportionate to the violation. Can it be challenged?

Yes, we help you challenge this decision relying on precise technical assessment showing the fine's disproportion to the actual violation.

We're planning to acquire an existing factory. Should we examine its historical environmental obligations?

We strongly recommend it; examining environmental obligations is an essential part of due diligence protecting you from inheriting undisclosed liability.
