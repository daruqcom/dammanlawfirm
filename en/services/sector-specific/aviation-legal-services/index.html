Aviation Legal Services

Aviation Legal Services in Dammam

Legal advisory for airlines and airport services operating in the Eastern Province, from ground service contracts to passenger claims and technical maintenance disputes.

Aviation Law Consultation on WhatsApp
Aviation legal services cover airport and ground-handling service contracts, passenger disputes with airlines over cancelled or delayed flights, and civil aviation regulatory compliance issued by relevant authorities. Eastern Province airports serve a large industrial sector relying on private aviation to move technical staff, making this legal field diverse between commercial and private aviation. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp.

Passenger Claims for Cancelled and Delayed Flights

We help passengers claim statutorily due compensation for cancelled or substantially delayed flights, under applicable passenger rights protection regulations, documenting the incident and filing the correct claim with the airline.

Ground-Handling and Catering Service Contracts for Airports

We review ground-handling and catering service contracts between airlines and service providers at Eastern Province airports, clarifying expected performance standards and compensation mechanisms upon breach of agreed service level.

Private Aviation Contracts Serving the Industrial Sector

We review private aviation contracts used to move technical staff between industrial project sites, including flight schedules, safety obligations, and cancellation or postponement terms protecting the lessee from unexpected financial risks.

Compliance With Civil Aviation Regulations

We help aviation operators, whether commercial or private, understand and comply with General Authority of Civil Aviation regulations, including operating license requirements and mandatory safety standards.

Lost or Damaged Baggage During Air Transport

We help passengers harmed by lost or damaged baggage during air transport claim due compensation per relevant international agreements and the airline's stated policies.

Flight Ticket Booking and Cancellation Disputes

We help passengers understand their rights when an airline cancels or modifies a flight booking, including the right to a refund or rebooking on an alternative flight without additional cost.

Aircraft and Ground Equipment Maintenance Contracts

We review aircraft and ground equipment maintenance contracts between aviation operators and approved maintenance service providers, ensuring clear quality standards and defined liability upon any malfunction.

Airline Liability for Passenger Injuries

We help passengers injured during a flight understand the airline's liability scope for these injuries and claim due compensation per relevant regulations and international agreements.

Technical Crew Licensing and Certificates of Airworthiness

We help aviation operators understand licensing requirements for technical and flight crews, including periodic validity certificates required by regulatory bodies to maintain flight operations safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I claim compensation for a cancelled flight?

Yes, under applicable passenger rights protection regulations, and we help you document the incident and file the correct claim with the airline.

Do you review private aviation service contracts for industrial companies?

Yes, we review private aviation contracts used to move technical staff between project sites, protecting the lessee from unexpected financial risks.

My baggage was lost during a flight. What are my options?

We help you claim due compensation per international agreements and the airline's stated policies.

The airline canceled my booking without sufficient notice. What are my rights?

You have the right to a refund or rebooking on an alternative flight without additional cost, and we help you claim this right.

I was injured during a flight due to crew negligence. Does the airline bear liability?

Yes, we help you understand this liability's scope and claim compensation per relevant regulations and international agreements.

We operate a private aviation fleet serving our industrial sites. Do we need a maintenance contract review?

We strongly recommend it, and help you review maintenance contracts to ensure clear quality standards and defined liability for any malfunction.

We operate a small airline. What are the licensing requirements for our technical crews?

We help you understand periodic validity certificates required by regulatory bodies to maintain your operations' safety.
