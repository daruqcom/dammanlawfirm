Real Estate & Construction Legal Services

Legal coverage for construction contracts and property ownership, tied to the reality of energy and infrastructure projects across the Eastern Province.

Real estate and construction law in Dammam covers construction contracts, real estate developer disputes, title deeds and land matters, and public projects and PPP arrangements — a critical area for any construction project linked to the region's energy or industrial sector. If you have a construction contract or a property dispute, the next step is reaching out directly on WhatsApp.

Why Construction in the Eastern Province Requires Special Legal Expertise

Building and development projects in the Eastern Province range from residential towers in Dammam to massive refineries in Ras Tanura to industrial complexes in Jubail, and each project type carries different legal risks requiring specialized expertise in construction and engineering.

Construction Contracts From Drafting to Execution

We accompany clients from the moment of initial contract drafting, through negotiation stages, to following actual execution on the project site, including handling any modifications or disputes arising during the work.

Property Ownership as the Foundation of Any Development Project

Before discussing construction, land ownership must be confirmed sound, which we cover under our real estate and property services, to avoid building an entire project on a fragile legal foundation.

Lease Disputes for Commercial and Residential Property

After a project is completed, a new phase of legal needs begins around leasing, and we help both owners and tenants with rental disputes registered through the Ejar platform.

Off-Plan Sales and Buyer Protection

For developers working on off-plan sale projects, we help meet Wafi system requirements, and help buyers verify any project's licensing before financial commitment, under our off-plan sales services.

Real Estate Financing and Mortgages as Part of the Equation

Most major real estate transactions involve bank financing, and we help clients understand mortgage obligations tied to this financing before signing any contract.

Real Estate Developer Disputes and Post-Delivery Warranties

We represent both buyers and developers in real estate developer disputes, from delayed delivery to specifications differing from what was agreed, among the most recurring disputes in major residential projects across the Eastern Province.

Public Projects and Partnerships With Government Bodies

For contractors working on government infrastructure projects, we help review public projects and PPP contracts and understand the long-term contractual obligations tied to this type of project.

Title Deed and Land Ownership Disputes

We help resolve title deed and land disputes arising from overlapping boundaries or multiple claimants to a single plot, a common issue across the Eastern Province's sprawling industrial lands.

Real Estate Brokerage and Its Role in Major Deals

We also review real estate brokerage agreements to precisely determine commission entitlement, especially in deals involving more than one broker.

Our Most Important Advice for Any Party in a Real Estate Deal

Never rely on personal trust alone in a major real estate transaction, however trustworthy the other party seems. An independent legal review isn't a sign of suspicion — it's sound professional practice followed by every successful real estate investor.

Our Services Don't Stop After the Contract Is Signed

We stay with our clients even after completing any real estate deal or construction project, confirming all contractual obligations are executed as agreed, and stepping in quickly if any later disagreement arises over execution or quality.
