Real Estate Developer Disputes

Real Estate Developer Disputes Lawyers in Dammam

Representing buyers and developers in disputes over delivery, specifications, and contractual obligations, from delayed delivery to hidden-defect warranties.

Developer Dispute Consultation on WhatsApp
Real estate developer disputes usually involve delivery delayed beyond the agreed date, actual specifications differing from what was agreed in the contract, or breach of post-delivery warranty obligations. Whether you're buyers seeking your right to fair compensation or developers defending your project against unwarranted claims, understanding this dispute type's contractual and statutory framework is essential. The next step is reaching out on WhatsApp.

Delivery Delayed Beyond the Agreed Date

We represent buyers harmed by their units' delivery being delayed beyond the date set in the sale contract, and help them claim due compensation per delay clauses stipulated in the contract.

Terminating the Purchase Contract Due to Serious Delay

When the delay is serious and unjustified, we help buyers assess their right to entirely terminate the purchase contract and claim a refund of paid amounts, plus compensation for any additional damage resulting from this delay.

Actual Specifications Differing From What Was Agreed

We help buyers who received units whose actual specifications differ from what they were promised in the contract or marketing catalog claim correction or compensation for this discrepancy.

Hidden-Defect Warranty After Delivery

We help buyers claim their right to a hidden-defect warranty for defects appearing after delivery, a recurring issue in major residential and commercial projects across the Eastern Province, determining the applicable warranty period per the contract and relevant regulations.

Defending Developers Against Unwarranted Claims

We represent developers in defending against buyer claims that might be unwarranted, or resulting from misunderstanding contract terms, interpreting these terms in a legally correct way protecting the developer's interest.

Execution Quality and Materials Used Disputes

We help buyers harmed by poor execution quality or use of materials violating agreed specifications document these violations with independent technical reports supporting their claim.

Off-Plan Sale Contracts and Their Associated Guarantees

We help buyers in off-plan sale contracts understand guarantees specific to this contract type, especially regarding protecting advance payments before the project is actually completed.

Homeowners Association and Shared Facility Management Disputes

We help resolve shared facility management disputes in major residential projects, including periodic maintenance fees and the developer's obligations toward these facilities after delivery.

Developer Liability for Structural Construction Defects

We help buyers claim their right to an extended warranty for serious structural construction defects, which are usually subject to a longer warranty period than ordinary finishing defects under applicable regulations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the hidden-defect warranty after delivery?

This is set by contract terms and relevant regulations — we review your specific contract to clarify the applicable period.

Can a purchase contract be terminated due to delayed delivery?

In certain cases the buyer is entitled to terminate and claim compensation — we assess this based on the contract terms and actual delay duration.

I received a unit whose area differs from what's stated in the contract. What are my options?

We help you claim correction or compensation for this discrepancy based on the original contract's terms.

I discovered an execution defect a year after delivery. Am I still covered by warranty?

It depends on the warranty period defined in your contract; we precisely review this period to determine your actual entitlement.

We're developers and a buyer is claiming compensation we believe is unwarranted. How do we defend our project?

We precisely review the contract's terms and help you interpret them in a legally correct way protecting your interest as developers.

I paid an advance amount in an off-plan project and its execution stopped. What are my options?

We help you understand guarantees specific to this type of contract and claim a refund of your payment or appropriate legal follow-up.

We discovered a structural crack in the building three years after delivery. Are we still covered by warranty?

Serious structural defects are usually subject to a longer warranty period than finishing defects, and we review your contract to determine your actual entitlement.
